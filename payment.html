Payment Options

Paypal

To pay with Paypal, simply select any product and click on the "Pay" button. Continue through all the steps and you will be prompted to login to your Paypal account.

Credit Card / Debit Card

To use your card for payment, simply select any product and click on the "Pay" button. Then proceed with Paypal even if you do not have an account. When prompted to login to paypal, underneath "Log In" there will be an option to "Pay with Credit/Debit Card" or "Continue as Guest". Select either one and continue through the remaining prompts.

Cash App, Google Pay, etc.

If you would like to purchase a product using any other form of payment, please email introshop@captainnick88.com and specify which form of payment you would like to use and we will get back to you as soon as possible.
